Why QRForge

QRForge runs fully in your browser, so your links and contact data are never uploaded. It supports URL, text, WiFi, email, and vCard formats with PNG/SVG export.

Popular QR Use Cases

Use QRForge as a free QR code generator for restaurant menus, WiFi sharing cards, event check-ins, digital business cards, and social profile links. You can also create a WiFi QR code for guests and a vCard QR code for quick contact sharing.
